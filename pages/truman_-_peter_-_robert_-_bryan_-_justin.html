STHS ART SHOW 2016

Truman - Peter - Robert - Bryan - Justin

Video Project

Artist's Statement

Our video has taken an optimistic outlook on the cycle of violence that’s clearly present in Child abuse. In our work, we depict a scenario where the young child’s belligerent father takes out his undying rage on his innocent child. On a deeper note, we showcase the stereotypical abuse children receive on a daily basis. I feel we can spread awareness by giving more attention to it.
